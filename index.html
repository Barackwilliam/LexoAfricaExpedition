LexoAfrica Expedition and Safaris is a locally-owned tour company based in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania. We are passionate about showcasing the breathtaking beauty, diverse wildlife, and rich cultures of East Africa through our expertly crafted tours and adventures. Whether you're seeking the thrill of an African safari, the challenge of a mountain trek, the tranquility of Zanzibar’s pristine beaches, or the warmth of cultural immersion, we tailor experiences to suit every traveler’s dream.

Our safari adventures take you deep into Tanzania’s iconic national parks, where you can witness the majestic Big Five, the Great Wildebeest Migration, and stunning landscapes that stretch as far as the eye can see. For those who crave adventure, our guided mountain treks offer the chance to conquer Africa’s highest peaks, including the legendary Mount Kilimanjaro and Mount Meru. Culture enthusiasts can engage with local communities, experiencing the traditions, cuisine, and hospitality that make Tanzania unique. And for those seeking relaxation, Zanzibar’s crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant history provide the perfect tropical escape.

At LexoAfrica, we pride ourselves on our exceptional service, knowledgeable guides, and commitment to responsible tourism. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring your journey is safe, seamless, and truly unforgettable. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple on a romantic getaway, a family on vacation, or a group of friends seeking adventure, we are here to create a once-in-a-lifetime experience tailored just for you. Join us and explore the wonders of Africa with LexoAfrica Expedition and Safaris—where every journey is an adventure, and every moment is a memory waiting to be made!
