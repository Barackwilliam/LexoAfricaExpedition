Safari Overview

Get yourself a chance to  explore 3 of the  best and most amazing National Parks in the Northern part of Tanzania, Serengeti National Park:The park  known for the highest concentration of animals, the only location in Africa where vast land animal Migrations still take place, Great Migration(Wildebeest Migration),

Ngorongoro Crater: is the home to the highest concentration of animals, where you can spot BIG FIVE animals, is one of the world’s top tourist destinations, also listed by UNESCO as a world heritage site, Tarangire national park: The park is known for the highest concentration of BIG heards of Elephants and Big Ancient Baobab trees, the park is the popular destination because of its unique attractions.

We usually recommend to our clients to do a hot air balloon safari whilst your in the Serengeti, A balloon ride in the Serengeti is quite an extraordinary adventure experience, imagine floating in a hot air balloon over the endless plains, offering a unique perspective, great view and photographic opportunity of the amazing scenery and wildlife.

Day By Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival At The Kilimanjaro International Airport/Arusha Airport-Arusha.

Upon your arrival at the Kilimanjaro/Arusha airport, you will be welcomed by MAASAI LADY TOURS ADVENTURE Professional Tour Guide, you will start heading to  Arusha Town for relaxation, dinner, and overnight while preparing Yourself for the incredible African adventure.

Day 2

Tarangire National Park.

After breakfast, we depart to the Tarangire national park for the full-day game drive, in the Tarangire will get to witness huge herds of BIG Elephants, and the BIGGEST BAOBAB TREES, all around the park, Elephants can be seen around the Tarangire River as they come for a wallow or drink, there are over 3000 Elephants in Tarangire, apart from elephants other animals can be seen is BIG CAT, S like leopards, lions, cheetahs, beautiful birds, elegant Giraffes, painted donkeys(zebra), warthogs(Pumba),  and other more, late afternoon will leave the park heading to Maasai boma/village to experience the unique culture of this iconic tribe in Africa, after Maasai, will leave heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner and overnight.

Day 3

Serengeti National Park.

After breakfast we depart to the Serengeti national park via Ngorongoro Conservation Area for full day game drive with packed lunch boxes, The Serengeti:The endless plains and abundance of wildlife animals, you will get to see  a lot of animals like leopards, cheetahs, lions, large groups of wildebeests, gazelles, warthog, elegant Giraffes, painted zebras, and many other beautiful animals across the endless plains of the Serengeti.

Day 4

Serengeti National Park-Park-Ngorongoro Conservation Area.

After breakfast we will leave the lodge, to continue with our remarkable adventure across the endless plains of one of Africa’s most iconic parks, around the park you will get to see different species of beautiful animals with their unique appearances, like painted zebras, wildebeests, warthogs, elands, painted dogs, lions, leopards, antelope, gazelles, and so many other animals, Late afternoon will exit heading to the Ngorongoro conservation area for relaxation, dinner and overnight.

Day 5

Ngorongoro Crater-Kilimanjaro/Arusha Airport.

After breakfast, You will leave the lodge heading to the Amazing Ngorongoro Crater, Listed as one of Africa’s  natural wonders by UNESCO due to its natural beauty and breathtaking views,

Ngorongoro Crater known as home to the vast, volcanic Ngorongoro Crater is the best place to spot the  “big 5” animals, also other animals lions pride, leopard, buffalo, hippos, and the rarely black rhino, Huge herds of wildebeest, zebras, warthogs, you will able to witness amazing view from the Ngorongoro Crater, You will enjoy the game until the mid-afternoon you will descend from the crater, heading to Arusha/Kilimanjaro Airport for your flight to Home/Zanzibar Island

Is Serengeti Balloon Safari Worth It

Absolutely! Here are a few reasons why you should book this unforgettable experience:

1. Spectacular Views:

Imagine yourself floating/soaring high above the Serengeti plains in a hot air balloon, witnessing the breathtaking sunrise, and getting a bird’s eye view of the sprawling savannah. The panoramic views of the vast grasslands, rolling hills, and diverse wildlife below are truly mesmerizing.

2. Wildlife Encounters:

: Serengeti National Park is renowned for its abundant wildlife, including the annual Great Migration. During your balloon safari, you have the opportunity to witness herds of wildebeests, zebras, giraffes, lions, elephants, and many other species in their natural habitat. The silence of the hot air balloon ensures a peaceful and undisturbed experience.

3. Memorable Experience:

A Serengeti Balloon Safari offers a unique and memorable experience that will stay with you for a lifetime. Floating above the African wilderness, you will feel a sense of awe and tranquility that is hard to replicate. It’s a perfect mix of adventure, luxury, and serenity.

4. Safety First:

Safety is a top priority during a Serengeti Balloon Safari. The operators maintain strict safety protocols and follow international standards to ensure your well-being throughout the entire experience. You can relax and enjoy the ride, knowing that you are in safe hands.

So, don’t hesitate to book your Serengeti Balloon Safari today and embrace the adventure of a lifetime.
