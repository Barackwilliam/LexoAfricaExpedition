Day By Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival At The Kilimanjaro International Airport/Arusha Airport-Arusha.

Upon your arrival at the Kilimanjaro/Arusha airport, you will be welcomed by MAASAI LADY TOURS &SAFARIS Professional Tour Guide, you’ll start heading to Arusha Town for relaxation, dinner, and overnight while preparing yourself for the incredible African adventure, during your safari we require you to be free on asking questions to your expert guide, and explaining your expectations during your safari so that you can get to learn/discover more about our amazing destinations.

Day 2

Tarangire National Park.

After breakfast we depart to the Tarangire National Park for an amazing full-day game drive, in the park you will get to see huge herds of Elephants and the biggest ancient Baobab trees all around the park, Elephants can be seen around the Tarangire River as they come for a wallow or drink, there are over 30,00 Elephants in the park, apart from elephants and ancient Baobab trees other species of animals can be seen like Lions pride (if your luck enough), leopards, cheetahs, hyenas, gazelles, antelopes, painted donkeys (zebra), warthogs (pumba), elegant Girrafes, beautiful birds also each species Has their own uniqueness, late afternoon will exit the park heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner, and overnight. 

Day 3

Serengeti National Park.

After breakfast we will leave the lodge heading to the serengeti national park via Ngorongoro Conservation Area for the game drive, the park is the best and most popular destination to spot BIG FIVE animals, if you are luck enough you can spot leopards, lions prides, a rarely black rhino,  other big five that can be seen is buffaloes and Elephants, the park Is famous of the annual Great Migration (Wildebeest Migration) also the park has the highest concentration of wildlife animals, and each species of animal have their uniqueness, you can see painted dogs, painted donkeys (zebra), dik-dik’s, elands, topis, hartebeests, impalas, and so many other animals, late afternoon will start heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 4

Serengeti National Park.

After breakfast we will leave the lodge for the incredible full-day game drive, the park is the best and popular destination to spot BIG FIVE animals, if your luck enough you can spot leopards, lions prides, a rarely black rhino,  other big five that can be seen is buffaloes and Elephants, the park Is famous of the annual Great Migration (Wildebeest Migration) also the park has a highest concentration of wildlife animals, and each species of animal have their own uniqueness, you can see painted dogs, painted donkeys (zebra), dik-dik’s, elands, topis, hartebeests, impalas and so many other animals, late afternoon will start heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 5

Serengeti National Park-Park-Ngorongoro Conservation Area.

After breakfast will proceed with our amazing adventure in the Serengeti plains, you can spot BIG FIVE animals, if you are lucky enough can spot lions,  leopards, and rarely black rhinos, other animals that can be seen around are buffaloes, elephants, painted dogs, gazelles, antelopes, hyenas and so many, late afternoon will exit the park heading to the Ngorongoro conservation area for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 6

Ngorongoro Crater.

After breakfast we will leave the lodge heading to Descend to the bottom of the crater for the full-day game drive, the crater is home to the highest concentration of wildlife animals, like buffaloes, warthogs (pumba), painted donkeys (zebras), wildebeest, hippopotamus, a rarely black rhino, beautiful birds lesser flamingos, also crater is one of best destinations to spot BIG FIVE animals, crater listed as one of the Africa’s natural wonders, because of its unique natural beauty, and stunning view, late afternoon will ascend from the crater heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 7

Lake Manyara National Park.

After breakfast we will drive to our final destination of this remarkable adventure, lake manyara national park, is a relatively small but diverse park, is named for the shallow salt lake that covers some seventy percent of the surface area, flooding and drying of the seasons, home to thousands of flamingos and in the region of extraordinary of more than 500 other bird species, on our exploration of the park you will see troop’s of baboon’s, monkeys, elegant Girrafes, painted donkey’s (zebras), the park is home to tree climbing lions, and tree climbing python’s, if your lucky enough you can spot them in our game drive, after lunch, we will leave the park heading to the accommodation for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 8

Mto Wa Mbu-Arusha

After breakfast we will leave the lodge heading to the Maasai village/boma for a culture tour, Maasai tribe is one of the most iconic tribe in Africa, they have got unique culture, appearance and lifestyle, Maasai women are very creative on making different attractive stuffs by using natural stuff like strong and unique colourful plants, strong soil by making beautiful pots, and so many things will also get a chance to visit their shops(Maasai shops) to support them, their shops are fully of beautiful stuff when you Visit in their shops you will get to know and to see more which will leave speechless by its beauty, late afternoon will leave Mto-wa Mbu to Arusha Town for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 9

Meru Forest Waterfalls

After breakfast, we will leave the lodge to Meru Forest for hiking, and enjoy the waterfalls while experiencing different attractions that are found around the forest like monkeys, beautiful birds, flowers and so many more…will enjoy our day until late afternoon we will start driving back to the Town for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 10

Materuni Village (Coffee Tour)

After breakfast we will leave the lodge heading to the Kilimanjaro region, Moshi District for the local tour, hiking, and waterfalls, while your in Materuni you will get a chance to make your own coffee and also will get a chance to taste local food from chaga tribe, will hike, and visit a waterfall around Materuni, late afternoon will start driving back to Arusha Town for relaxation, dinner, and overnight.

Day 11

Chemka Hotspring(Kikuketwa)

After breakfast we will leave the lodge heading to the Moshi District, Chemka Hot Springs is an underground crystal clear spring located in an area called Rundugai which is between Arusha and Moshi.

This is also known as Kikuletwa hotspring it originates from the Kilimanjaro mountain. This is a wonderful place for relaxation, various Games, swimming, camping, and fun activities, you will also enjoy the tasty barbecue of Tanzania, also around Chemka some animals can be seen like monkeys, and beautiful birds, late afternoon will start driving back to Arusha Town for relaxation, dinner and overnight.

Day 12

Arusha-Kilimanjaro Airport/Arusha Airport.

After breakfast we will leave the lodge for the Town tour around around City (depend on your  departure flight time), mid-afternoon we will leave Arusha city to the Kilimanjaro airport/Arusha Airport for a departure flight to home or Zanzibar Island

Is Serengeti Balloon Safari Worth It

Absolutely! Here are a few reasons why you should book this unforgettable experience:

1. Spectacular Views:

Imagine yourself floating/soaring high above the Serengeti plains in a hot air balloon, witnessing the breathtaking sunrise, and getting a bird’s eye view of the sprawling savannah. The panoramic views of the vast grasslands, rolling hills, and diverse wildlife below are truly mesmerizing.

2. Wildlife Encounters:

: Serengeti National Park is renowned for its abundant wildlife, including the annual Great Migration. During your balloon safari, you have the opportunity to witness herds of wildebeests, zebras, giraffes, lions, elephants, and many other species in their natural habitat. The silence of the hot air balloon ensures a peaceful and undisturbed experience.

3. Memorable Experience:

A Serengeti Balloon Safari offers a unique and memorable experience that will stay with you for a lifetime. Floating above the African wilderness, you will feel a sense of awe and tranquility that is hard to replicate. It’s a perfect mix of adventure, luxury, and serenity.

4. Safety First:

Safety is a top priority during a Serengeti Balloon Safari. The operators maintain strict safety protocols and follow international standards to ensure your well-being throughout the entire experience. You can relax and enjoy the ride, knowing that you are in safe hands.

So, don’t hesitate to book your Serengeti Balloon Safari today and embrace the adventure of a lifetime.
